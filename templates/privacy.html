Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create a Spendly account and use the service, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: your name and email address, provided at registration.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data: pages visited and actions taken within the app, used to improve the service.

We do not collect payment information. Spendly is free to use and does not process any financial transactions on your behalf.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to provide and improve Spendly. Specifically:

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To display your expense history and summaries to you.
  • To identify and fix bugs or performance issues.
  • To send essential service communications, such as password reset emails.

We will never sell your personal data or use it for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We apply industry-standard technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon request, your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal or financial data with third parties, except in the following limited circumstances:

  • Hosting providers: we use third-party infrastructure to host the application. These providers process data only on our behalf and are bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal requirements: we may disclose data if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal process.

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. Cookies

Spendly uses a single session cookie to keep you logged in between page visits. This cookie contains no personal information and is deleted when you log out or close your browser. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to request access to or deletion of your data, please reach out through the contact information provided within the app. We will respond to all privacy-related requests within 14 days.
